Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our cycling data tracking hobby project, which utilizes GPX files and integrates with the Strava API.

Information We Collect

GPX Files When you upload GPX files, we collect: Route information GPS coordinates Time stamps Elevation data Strava Integration When you connect your Strava account, we access: Profile information (name, profile picture) Cycling activity details (routes, distances, durations, timestamps) We do not store your Strava login credentials.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to: Provide insights into your cycling activities Visualize your cycling routes and performance Improve our hobby project features and functionality Sharing Your Information We do not share your personal data with third parties, except: To comply with legal obligations To enforce our policies or protect our rights Your data from Strava and GPX files is never sold or distributed for commercial purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, as this is a hobby project, please avoid uploading sensitive or highly confidential information.

Your Rights

You can: Request access to your personal data Request deletion or correction of your data Disconnect your Strava account at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
